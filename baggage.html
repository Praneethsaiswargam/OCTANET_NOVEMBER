Baggage Requirements

At Oceanic Airlines, we want to ensure that your journey is comfortable and hassle-free. Please review our baggage requirements below:

Carry-On Baggage

Passengers are allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item (e.g., purse, laptop bag) in the cabin. Please note the following requirements:

  • Maximum dimensions: 22 x 14 x 9 inches (56 x 36 x 23 cm).
  • Total weight must not exceed 15 lbs (7 kg).

Checked Baggage

Checked baggage allowances vary depending on your ticket type and destination. Please refer to your booking details or contact our customer service for specific information.
